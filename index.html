SSVM World School began with a spark of thought in 2008 that soon grew into a reality within a few years since inception. Inaugurated on 10th July 2010, SSVM World School is a CBSE Schools in Coimbatore offers a systematic blend of innovative teaching methodology, steady progression, and spirituality in education. The school maintains the strength of 2000 students(Teacher-Student ratio 25:1). The school is co-educational and affiliated to CBSE, Delhi with English as a medium of instruction.
